# Delete Your Account

You can request the deletion of your MotoWrench account and all associated data at any time.

## How to request account deletion

1. Send an email to [dr650@sozora.com](mailto:dr650@sozora.com) with the subject line **"Delete my account"**.
2. Use the email address associated with your MotoWrench account.
3. We will confirm deletion by reply.

## What gets deleted

| Data type | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Account profile (name, email, user icon) | Deleted |
| Saved vehicles and maintenance logs | Deleted |
| App feedback | Deleted |
| Anonymous, aggregated analytics | Retained (not linked to you) |

## Timeline

Account deletion is processed within **30 days** of the request. Once complete, the action is irreversible.
