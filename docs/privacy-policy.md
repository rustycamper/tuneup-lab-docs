# MotoWrench Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 18, 2026*

## Overview

MotoWrench is a motorcycle maintenance tracker. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

## Data We Collect

- **Account information:** When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email address, display name, and profile photo URL. Anonymous (guest) users do not provide any personal information.
- **Service records:** Motorcycle details (nickname, odometer readings), maintenance history, service photos, and notes you enter in the app.
- **Crash and diagnostic data:** We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic information to improve app stability.
- **Analytics:** We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used in aggregate. This data is not linked to your identity.

## How We Use Your Data

- **Sync:** Your service records are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) so you can access them across devices.
- **AI Mechanic:** When you ask the AI Mechanic a question, your query and relevant bike context are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a response. Queries are not stored beyond the session.
- **Crash reporting:** Crash data helps us identify and fix bugs.
- **Analytics:** Aggregate usage data helps us improve the app experience.

## Third-Party Services

MotoWrench uses the following third-party services:

- **Google Firebase:** Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Storage, Crashlytics, Analytics, and AI (Gemini). See [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).
- **Google Sign-In:** For authentication. See [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Apple Sign-In:** For authentication. See [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase servers. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Service photos are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from **Settings > Delete Account** within the app. This permanently removes your account, all garage and service records, and all photos from our servers.

## Children's Privacy

MotoWrench is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us through the app in Settings -> Feedback.
